Matcha Latte

Description

A milk based green tea drink offered iced or hot.

Ingredients

  • 2 Teaspoons matcha powder
  • 1/8 Cups hot water
  • Ice
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Scoop 2 teaspoons matcha powder into your matcha bowl.
  2. Pour 1/8 cups hot water into bowl and whisk with chasen until smooth.
  3. Fill glass with ice.
  4. Pour desired amount of milk to fill glass.
  5. Spoon matcha mixture on top.
  6. Give it a stir and enjoy!
