Breakfast Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Croissant
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Slice cheddar cheese
  • 1 Piece cooked bacon

Steps

  1. Heat pan to medium high and cook bacon until done.
  2. Drain desired amount of fat from pan.
  3. Whisk in egg to scramble until done.
  4. Make horizontal cut in croissant.
  5. Add eggs, bacon, and cheese.
  6. Toast in oven until cheese is melted.
  7. Remove from oven and enjoy!
