Onigiri

Description

Onigiri is a Japanese rice ball made from white rice. It is usually formed into triangular or cylindrical shapes, and wrapped in nori. Onigiri traditionally have sour or salty fillings such as salted salmon.

Ingredients

  • rice
  • water
  • salt
  • nori
  • filling

Steps

  1. Wash rice and drain.
  2. Fill pot with 2:1 water - rice.
  3. Bring to boil and stir.
  4. Simmer on low covered for 20 minutes.
  5. Meanwhile prepare your filling.
  6. Cut nori into strips.
  7. Once rice is cooked, cool and place ~1/2 cup rice into palm.
  8. Put filling in center and mold rice over filling to form triangle shape.
  9. Fold nori strips on outside of rice to finish onigiri.
